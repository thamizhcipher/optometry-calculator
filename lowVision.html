Magnification Calculator(Distance)

Note:Provide the Present Visual Acuity and Required Visual Acuity to calculate the Magnification for Distance
Instructions: Select the Present Visual Acuity and Required Visual Acuity from the dropdown menu, then click 'Calculate'.



Formula

  • Magnification =(Required Acuity / Present Acuity)

Magnification Calculator(Near)

Note:Provide the Present Near Visual Acuity and Required Near Visual Acuity to calculate the Magnification for Near
Instructions:Select the 'Present Near Visual Acuity' and 'Required Near Visual Acuity' from the dropdown menus, then click 'Calculate'

Visual Acuity

Formula:

  • Magnification =(Present Near Visual Acuity / Required Near Visual Acuity)

Kestenbaum Rule Calculator

Note:Provide the (BCVA) Best Corrected Visual Acuity for distance to calculate the magnification for near
Instructions:Select the BCVA from the dropdown menu and click 'Calculate'.

Formula:

  • Magnification = 1 / (BCVA)

    • Kestenbaum Rule: Followed to estimate the near add for initial trial based on Best Corrected Visual Acuity(BCVA) for distance.
