Vertex Distance Effective Power Calculator

Note: Provide a spherical or spherocylinder prescription to calculate the effective power.

Instructions: Choose the conversion (Specs to CL / CL to Specs), Provide the prescription, and then click 'Calculate'.

Select conversion:



Result:

Formula:

DE = DL / (1 - d * DL)

  • DE = Effective Power(D)
  • DL = Lens Power(D)
  • d = Vertex Distance(m)

Keratometry Base Curve

Note:Provide the dioptric power of average K to calculate the Base Curve
Instructions: Enter the average K (D) and click 'Calculate'.

How it calculates ?

  • Base Curve(mm) = 337.5 / Average K (D)

LARS Rule Calculator

Note:Provide the initial axis and the axis after blinking to apply the LARS rule
Instructions: Enter the initial axis before blinking and the axis after blinking, then click 'Apply LARS'.

Inputs must be in the range of 0 to 180.

Expand of LARS(CAAS)

  • L - Left (Clockwise)
  • A - Add
  • R - Right(Anti-Clockwise)
  • S - Substract
