Vertex Distance Effective Power Calculator

Note: Select (sphere to cyl) or (cyl to sphere) based on your requirement

Instructions: It can calculate vertex distance for spherical and spherocylinderical prescription.

Result:

Formula: DE = DL / (1 - d * DL), where DE is Effective Power, DL is Lens Power, and d is Vertex Distance.

Radius of Curvature to Dioptric Power Calculator

Calculations happen automatically. Enter either the Radius of Curvature (in mm) or the Dioptric Power, and both values will be displayed simultaneously

Formula

  • Dioptric Power = 337.5 / Radius of Curvature
  • Radius of Curvature = 337.5 / Dioptric Power
